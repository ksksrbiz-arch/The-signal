# Daily Script Blueprints

Every monologue uses the **Stoic Formula**:

1. **Cold Truth** — a cynical observation about the world (the hook).
2. **Silent War** — the lonely nature of the build (the friction).
3. **Dominance** — short, punchy statement of power (the resolution).

Aim for ~15–25 seconds total, ~45–65 words. Read slow. Pause hard.

---

## Blueprint 1 — The Comfortable Lie

**Cold Truth.** Most people don't want the truth. They want a comfortable lie they can repeat at dinner.

**Silent War.** So I stopped explaining. Stopped pitching. Stopped asking permission to build the thing they couldn't see yet.

**Dominance.** Let them stay comfortable. I'll take the rest.

> **Visual cue:** Open on Asset #1 (The Operator). Cut to Asset #3 (Rain Window) on "stopped explaining." Hold on a beat of silence before "Let them stay comfortable."

## Blueprint 2 — The City That Doesn't Care

**Cold Truth.** The city doesn't care if you're tired. The market doesn't care if it's fair. The clock doesn't care if you're ready.

**Silent War.** Nobody is coming to save the build. There's just the work, the room, and whoever's still awake at 2 a.m.

**Dominance.** I'm still awake.

> **Visual cue:** Open on Asset #5 (Rooftop, skyline). Push in slowly. Cut to Asset #2 (Midnight Grind) on "still awake at 2 a.m." Black frame on the last line.

---

## Blueprint 3 — The Quiet Build

**Cold Truth.** The loudest people in the room are usually selling the smallest things.

**Silent War.** Every empire I've ever respected was built in silence — by someone who couldn't afford to be wrong, and didn't bother being heard.

**Dominance.** Build quiet. Land loud.

> **Visual cue:** Asset #10 (Brand Carrier) cold open. Stay on the face. No B-roll. Let the silence do the work. Hex-glow anchor visible behind the shoulder.

---

## Blueprint 4 — The Mirror

**Cold Truth.** You're not behind. You're just early to a fight nobody else has noticed yet.

**Silent War.** The doubt isn't a signal to stop. It's the toll you pay for seeing something the rest of the room missed.

**Dominance.** Pay the toll. Keep walking.

> **Visual cue:** Asset #3 (Rain Window) — start on the reflection, rack focus to the face on "Pay the toll." Final beat: teal-to-cyan color flash on "Keep walking."

---

## Blueprint 5 — The Long Game

**Cold Truth.** Everyone wants the result. Almost nobody wants the seven years it takes to look like an overnight success.

**Silent War.** The grind isn't loud. It's not motivational. It's a decision you make every morning that nobody is going to clap for.

**Dominance.** Clap or don't. I'm not stopping.

> **Visual cue:** Asset #7 (Laptop Light) cold open — extreme close-up, underlit. Cut to Asset #8 (City Grid Overhead) on "Clap or don't." Hold final frame for 2 full seconds.

## Voice Direction (ElevenLabs)

- **Voice:** deep, gravelly, measured. Try `Marcus`, `Knight`, or a custom clone tuned low.
- **Stability:** 35–45% (lets weight and pauses through).
- **Similarity:** 75%+.
- **Style:** 0–15% (no theatrical lift; this is interior monologue, not performance).

## Audio Bed

- **Layer A:** lo-fi dark synth, solo melancholic piano, or muted trumpet.
- **Layer B:** city foley — distant siren, rain on pavement, server-room hum.
- **Silence:** cut everything for 0.5–1.0s before the **Dominance** line. Hits hardest.
