# Master Prompt Template

> `Photorealistic [SHOT TYPE] of [CHARACTER], [ACTION] in [ENVIRONMENT], lit by [LIGHTING SOURCE], [COLOR PALETTE], cinematic [LENS], ultra-sharp facial detail, 8K, dark city noir aesthetic, [TEXTURE KEYWORDS].`

Pick one cell from each column. Append a Visual Anchor. Done.

## The Noir Library

| SHOT TYPE | ACTION | ENVIRONMENT | LIGHTING SOURCE | COLOR PALETTE |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Extreme close-up | Adjusting a cufflink | Brutalist parking garage | Flickering blue neon tube | Teal & charcoal |
| Medium shot | Staring at a city grid | Dimly lit industrial loft | Single warm tungsten bulb | Indigo & electric cyan |
| Low angle (power) | Closing a leather folder | Rain-slicked balcony | Cold white monitor glow | Amber & deep black |
| Over-the-shoulder | Pouring a glass of water | Concrete subway platform | Red emergency exit light | Emerald & slate |
| Wide cinematic | Walking through mist | High-rise glass boardroom | Golden hour through blinds | Silver & midnight blue |
| Macro (detail) | Tapping a luxury watch | Hidden underground vault | Harsh moonlight through slats | Violet & obsidian |

## Lens Vocabulary

`85mm f/1.4`, `50mm cinematic`, `35mm anamorphic`, `macro lens`, `wide cinematic`.

## Texture Keywords (anti-plastic skin)

`fine skin pores`, `hyper-realistic skin texture`, `subtle perspiration`,
`micro-details`, `film grain`, `shot on 35mm Kodak Portra 400`.

## Visual Anchors (pick one and use it every reel)

1. **Geometric glow** — faint hexagonal light pattern on a wall or glass.
2. **Uniform** — black turtleneck or matte-black ring, always present.
3. **Color flash** — final beat shifts from teal to electric cyan.

## Aspect Ratios

- Reels / Shorts / TikTok: `--ar 9:16`
- Cinematic horizontal: `--ar 21:9` or `--ar 16:9`

## Worked Example

`Extreme close-up` + `adjusting a cufflink` + `brutalist parking garage` +
`flickering blue neon tube` + `teal & charcoal` + `85mm f/1.4` + hex-glow anchor:

> Photorealistic extreme close-up of a self-made entrepreneur, late 20s,
> adjusting a cufflink in a brutalist parking garage, lit by a single flickering
> blue neon tube overhead, teal and charcoal palette, cinematic 85mm f/1.4,
> ultra-sharp facial detail, 8K, dark city noir aesthetic, fine skin pores and
> film grain, faint hexagonal light pattern reflected on the concrete wall
> behind him. --ar 9:16
